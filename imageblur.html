Photoshop Field Blur

For this image, I used Photoshop’s Field Blur tool to keep the garbage bin in focus while softening the background and surroundings. The blur effect removes distractions, which makes the bin the clear subject of the photo. What’s interesting is how an everyday object we normally ignore suddenly becomes the center of attention. By blurring out everything else, the bin feels more significant than it would in real life.
